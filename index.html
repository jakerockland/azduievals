AZ DUI Evaluations

What We Do

When an individual loses their driver’s license due to a DUI, they must complete substance treatment. The law then requires that a licensed professional conduct an assessment to make a clinical recommendation to the MVD, for consideration of driver’s license reinstatement.

Liana Condello, MA, LISAC is an experienced state licensed evaluator available to meet the customer where they are at in order to complete and submit PART C of the MVD Revocation packet.
